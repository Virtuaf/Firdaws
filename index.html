FILIB: the future of reading

FILIB stands for FIction LIBrary. To create this app we had to answer the question, How do we organize books?
Organizing books digitally and physically permits two different initiatives when they have the same requirements
Thats why we created a digital library that helps you sort your books by theme, genre, author, length, popularity, fondness etc.
depending on your desires you can customize your shelf to look like it would in reality.
